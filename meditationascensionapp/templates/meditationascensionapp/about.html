About..

Thanks for travelling to my website. My background is in corporate events and conferencing where I worked for over 15 years at a variety of organisations. During my career, mindfulness and meditation helped me with workloads and deadlines. The events world is intense and one or the most stressful of industries: I had to manage multiple projects and juggle many plates.

Meditation had been in my life since childhood, with my Grandmother being my first influence. Although I used to laugh at her practices at the time and thought they were strange. However, having been through interesting relationships and life experiences with many obstacles, I came to a breaking point. It wasn't in my nature to let go easily, I found it very hard to walk away and give up on people, relationships, jobs, friends and family.

I'd experienced this difficulty over and over again until I found that letting things be and going with the flow was the right route for me. I had to release and surrender to what was. Also, pressure from work and life in general meant my health was suffering: I had digestion problems, asthma, and an under-active thyroid, which made me put on weight.

My body needed a rest. After I started yoga classes, I began to notice what my mind was doing while in the various postures. I began hot yoga a few years later - a moving meditation - which led me to the journey of meditating which gave me life altering experiences. I started to attend mindfulness retreats and it was there I met like-minded people who supported me to continue to meditate.
